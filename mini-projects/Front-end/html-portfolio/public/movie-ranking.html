The Best Marvel Movies According to Me

Top 3 Marvel Movies:

Avengers Infinity War

This movie brought all of our favorite superheroes together and provided us with the one thing nobody expected. Our beloved heroes losing.

Spiderman No Way Home

Every person in the past generations has looked up to a Spiderman, and seeing all 3 different eras of Spiderman coming together brought everyone together.

Thor Ragnarock

This movie embodied the humour, action, and plot of a classic superhero movie in superb fashion. It was enjoyable throughout set the stage for Inifinity War.
